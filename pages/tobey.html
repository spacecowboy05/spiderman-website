Tobey Maguire in Spider-Man

The first of this trilogy was released in 2002, all of them starring Tobey Maguire as Spider-Man. This was the first mainstream live-action adaptation of Spider-Man. It recieved very well among fans and critics. This film series served as many as their introduction to Spider-Man.

Kristen Dunst as Mary-Jane Watson was the primary heroine in all three movies.
