Tom Holland in Spider-Man

With Tom Holland as Spider-Man, this is the most recent live-action Spider-Man series. This film is more light-hearted than previous franchises, seeking to entertain a younger audience. This movie is also intergrated with Marvel's Cinematic Universe. Significantly, this series sees the re-appearance of many main stars from the previous franchises, including Andrew Garfield and Tobey Maguire, as well as the antagonists and original actors.

This franchise's heroine is Mary-Jane Watson played by Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman.

Main Antagonists throughout the Series (ordered by first significant appearance)

  2. Mysterio by Jake Gyllenhaal
  3. Doctor Octopus by Alfred Molina
  4. The Lizard by Rhys Ifans
  5. Green Goblin by Willem Dafoe
  6. Electro by Jamie Foxx
