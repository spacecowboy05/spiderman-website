Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko in 1962.

Spider-Man quickly grew in popularity, becoming one of the more recognizable images in global culture.

There have been 4 mainstream film adaptations of Spider-Man, with 3 live actions franchises and 1 animated franchise.

  • Spider-Man with Tobey Maguire
  • The Amazing Spider-Man with Andrew Garfield
  • Spider-Man with Tom Holland
  • Spider-Man (animated) with Shameik Moore
